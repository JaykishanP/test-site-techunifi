Drawing Mock-ups

Guest Rooms

Phone

  • Phone drops will be installed on the nightstand beside each bed.
  • All phone cables will be white.
  • Jack type: CAT3 Keystone Jack.

Data

  • Data cables will be placed either behind the TV or under the desk.
  • Cable color: Blue (Orange for renovation areas).
  • Jack type: CAT6 Keystone Jack.
  • Cable type: CAT6 riser (unless otherwise specified by the client).

TV

  • TV cables will be placed behind the TV.
  • Cable type: RG6Q.


Common Areas

Meeting Rooms

  • Each room will require one Ethernet and one HDMI connection.
  • Speakers are needed.

Desks (GM, Sales, Work Area)

  • Each desk will have 4 CAT6 drops nearby.

Front Desk

  • The front desk will require 6 CAT6 drops within the furniture.
  • Back of House (BOH)
  • Volume controls are needed for each zone.
  • Data cable required for the time clock.
  • Phone jack needed for the fax machine.
  • Access point needed.
  • Pool phone.
  • If necessary, speakers will be installed.
  • Business Center
  • 4 data points required for the printer and 2 computers.
  • Employee Breakroom
  • Pool
  • Speaker required.
  • Phone required.
  • Camera will be installed.


General Rules

  • Access Point Placement
  • APs should be placed every other room unless specified.
  • In common areas, access points should be roughly 30 feet apart.
  • Camera Placement
  • Cameras should cover all exits and common areas within the property.
  • Speaker Placement
  • Speakers should be installed in high-traffic areas such as meeting rooms, fitness room, lobby, breakfast area, vestibule, pool, common area bathrooms, elevator lobby, and employee breakroom.
  • Distance: 6 feet apart.


Color Code

  • Blue (new builds)/Orange (Renovations) - guest room
  • Purple - Common area
  • Green - Common area Access point
  • Yellow - Camera
  • White - Phone
  • Pink - Restaurant
