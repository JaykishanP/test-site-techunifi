Our Story

The founder had a vision of creating a service company that offers comprehensive low-voltage, IT, and smart technology turnkey solutions, with a customer-focused service model, for small and medium-sized businesses. Even though the founder had been leading the previously established company since 2006, the vision truly came to life in 2020 with the founding of Techunifi. Techunifi is a new company that prioritizes technical expertise and has the flexibility to deliver ever-evolving technologies. It aims to future-proof its solutions to shield clients from costly upgrades and replacements.

Leveraging more than 25 years of extensive experience within the technology industry, the founder meticulously established Techunifi with the vision of positioning it as the definitive one-stop destination for its clientele. With an unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation, Techunifi has seamlessly ingrained itself into the fabric of the industry, serving as a dependable technology partner to over 100 businesses nationwide within a brief span of just four years from its inception.

As we focus on the future, our commitment remains strong. Our goal is not just to maintain but to improve our path, always creating exceptional experiences, building lasting relationships, and advancing industry standards. How it Works

01

Design

During the design phase, we manage design narratives, conduct drawing reviews, oversee standards reviews, and coordinate and create shop drawings.

02

Construction

During the construction phase, we oversee structured cabling, manage ISP contracts, provide high-speed internet access, implement security measures, and assist with EV charging selection.

03

Install

During the installation phase, we handle IT Network closet builds, ensure certifications are obtained, set up PBX systems, install surveillance equipment, and integrate audio/video components.

04

Go Live

During the go-live phase, we facilitate integration and interface with property management systems (PMS), conduct training sessions, and provide comprehensive documentation to ensure smooth transition to operations.

05

Operations

In operations, we offer managed IT solutions, provide remote support, ensure 24/7 assistance, and oversee technology refresh initiatives to keep your systems up-to-date and running smoothly.
